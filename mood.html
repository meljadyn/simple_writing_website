Mood Boards

This is one strategy you can use to get an idea of the mood and atmosphere of a story, setting, or scene. You can also use mood boards more literally, such as to document people that you find look close to your characters or weapons.

These can be as literal or figurative as you wish. A roaring flame could be representative of a character's mental state. Honestly, whatever helps you set the tone of your story is good to go here. Don't overthink it! Here's a brief example of how I'd set one up.
